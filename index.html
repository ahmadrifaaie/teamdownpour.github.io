Welcome to Team DownPour's website.
Our team consists of Ahmad and Sorenon.

This website serves as a platform for documenting our works on our prototype.
Our prototype is a window tht closes on its own when it rains.
A brief summary on how the prototype works is that a moisture sensor detects rain and sends a signal to a mircocontroller. The mircocontroller will then cause the motor to turn, closing the windows.
